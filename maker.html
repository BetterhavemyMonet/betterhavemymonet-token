🛠️ MONET Maker

What is this?
Create your own level and challenge others.

How to play:
1. Tap squares to place obstacles
2. Click Save to store your level
3. Click Play to try it

Goal:
Avoid obstacles and survive as long as possible

💡 Soon: Players will pay MONET to play your levels
